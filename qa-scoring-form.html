Journey Outcome & Continuity Anchors
Journey Outcome (0–5)
5 — Resolved AND confirmed, positive close
4 — Resolved, minor friction
3 — Partial / accepted workaround with clear path
2 — Unresolved but customer holds a valid, owned next step
1 — Unresolved, customer disengaged
0 — Customer harmed, churned, or left with legal/compliance exposure
Continuity & Handoffs (0–5)
5 — Seamless
4 — Minor gaps, quickly recovered
3 — Some repeated work, thread held
2 — Significant rework from thin notes / wrong statuses
1 — Chain repeatedly broke
0 — Continuity collapse itself drove the complaint
